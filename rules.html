Learn to Play the Game!

Work as a team to complete one firework in each color.

A firework is complete when you play each value, in order, of that color.

Completing all the fireworks is tough, but possible.

You can see everyone's tiles but your own.

Give clues to other players to help them decide which tiles to play.

Rely on their clues to make the right moves...but clues are limited.

On your turn, you have four options:

  1. Play a tile if you think it will score.

    • Colors can be completed in any order.
    • Tile will score if it is next number for that color.

  2. Discard a tile.

    • Discarding a tile puts it out of the game.
    • But discarding a tile earns the team an extra hint (maximum 10).

  3. Give a color hint to another player.

    • Select one tile in another players hand and click, hint color.
    • The player will see that tile's color, and which tiles in the hand have the same color
    • Remember, your hints are limited (more can be earned by discarding)

  4. Give a value hint to another player.

    • Same as color hint, but shows the tiles' value

The info panel tells you what you need to know.

Tiles Left: How many tiles are in the draw pile.

Hints Left: How many hints can you give (discard to earn more).

Fuses Left: Lose a fuse every time you try to score and fail.

  • If you go into negative numbers, you lose the game!

The game ends when:

  1. You score all 25 tiles...a perfect score!
  2. You run out of fuses and fail another score...you lose!
  3. The draw pile runs out of tiles:
    • When the last tile is drawn, every player gets one last turn.
    • Then the score is added up, one point for every scored tile.
    • Any score under 16 brings shame to your family.

Good Luck!
